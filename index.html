A bat call will be relatively tonal and when played back will sound like a whistle.

Generally a bat call will be longer than 2.5ms and shorter than 70ms.

Generally bat calls are produced in a sequence (Note, however, that low frequency bat calls (<25kHz) may only produce a single call in a 320ms snapshot.

Social Play example

Social calls usually consist of a series of downward sweeping fequency modulated components - for example every 6ms.
